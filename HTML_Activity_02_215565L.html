Undergraduate courses

BSc.(Hons) in IT
The Honours Degree of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology is a four-year full-time undergraduate degree programme offered by the Department. The curriculum is anchored on the principles and fundamentals of Information Technology, making the students competitive both in academic studies and practice. The Department annually enrols 200 undergraduates for this Degree Programme.
BSc.(Hons) in IT & Management
The B.Sc. (Hons) in Information Technology & Management Degree Programme is conducted as a semester-based course unit system. The award of the degrees is based on the relevant By-Laws and the performance criteria. The course comprises of 7 semesters of academic work and a 24 week period of industrial training between the 5th and 6th semesters.
BSc.(Hons) in AI
The degree of BSc Hons (AI) produces graduates with in-depth knowledge in concepts, theory, industry applications and academic research in AI leading to devising symbolic and non-symbolic AI solutions for complex real-world problems exhibiting algorithmic and/or non-algorithmic nature in their behaviour. These graduates can visualize and conceptualize AI solutions for real-world problems that could not be solved otherwise, engage in self-learning, work in teams, secure high levels of professionalism, ensure ethical, legal, and social concerns in AI solutions, and effectively communicating about AI solutions to the society.
